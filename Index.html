Design System · v1.0

ShowUp

An editorial, calm system for an app that connects people through activity — not through friendship.

01

Colors

A deliberately small palette. The discipline is the design. The lime accent exists for a single purpose: to mark the moment when something becomes confirmed.

Surfaces

surface

#FAF8F3

Default page background. Warm off-white, never pure white.

surface-raised

#FFFFFF

Cards, inputs, modals. Anything elevated.

surface-sunken

#F2EFE8

Inset areas, dividers, subtle separation.

Ink — Text

ink

#1A1A1A

Primary text, headings, important UI.

ink-secondary

#4A4A4A

Body copy, descriptions, paragraphs.

ink-tertiary

#7A7A7A

Captions, metadata, helper text.

ink-muted

#A8A8A8

Placeholders, disabled states.

Accent — Reserved for confirmation

accent

#D1F471

Only for quorum reached, confirmation, momentum.

accent-soft

#E8FAB8

Subtle tint for success state backgrounds. Use even more sparingly.

Use it for

  • "Quorum reached" / "Confirmed" badge
  • QuorumIndicator fill bar when reached
  • Subtle focus rings (40% opacity)
  • A 4px dot beside an active item
  • Text selection

Don't use it for

  • Primary buttons (those use ink black)
  • Generic success states
  • Gradients or large filled areas
  • Logo or brand name styling
  • Anywhere without a confirmation meaning
02

Typography

The contrast between Fraunces (editorial serif) and Inter (functional sans) is ShowUp's visual voice. Hierarchy comes from weight and scale — never from color or boxes.

Display · Headings

Fraunces

Contemporary serif with display-optimized variations. Lends editorial gravity to titles without crossing into nostalgic territory.

Body · UI

Inter

Clean, neutral sans-serif. Keeps body copy calm, readable, and functional without competing with headings.
